Apple watch model requirement

Yeye Score WatchOS application runs on Apple Watch Series 6 and newer devices.

How to install the application

Simply open the App Store, find Yeye WatchOS application, and click the download button.

How to use the application

  • Tap the start button after opening Yeye WatchOS application on your apple watch.
  • While playing tennis, tap on the corresponding section to add score if one side wins and double tap to undo.
  • Swiping right and tap on End to end the session, Reset to clear the scores and Tie to enter the tie-break mode.
